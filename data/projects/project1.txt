Project: Digital Loan Approval System

Objective:
The objective of this project is to design and implement a fully digital loan approval platform that significantly reduces the time required to process personal loan applications while ensuring consistent and compliant decision-making.
The system should improve operational efficiency by automating repetitive and manual tasks currently performed by loan officers, such as data validation, credit assessment, and eligibility checks. At the same time, it must maintain a high level of accuracy and transparency in decision-making to comply with financial regulations and internal audit requirements.
Additionally, the solution should enhance customer experience by providing faster feedback, real-time application tracking, and a seamless digital journey from application submission to final decision.

Description:
Currently, the loan approval process relies heavily on manual review, fragmented systems, and inconsistent evaluation criteria. This leads to long processing times, increased operational costs, and potential human error in decision-making.
The proposed system aims to centralize and automate the end-to-end loan approval workflow. Customers will be able to submit their applications through a digital interface, providing necessary personal, financial, and employment information. The system will then process this data, apply predefined risk assessment rules, and generate a recommendation (approval, rejection, or manual review).
Loan officers will interact with the system through a dashboard that provides visibility into applications, risk scores, and supporting data. They should be able to override automated decisions when necessary, ensuring flexibility in complex cases.
The platform must also maintain detailed audit logs of all decisions and actions taken, ensuring traceability and compliance with regulatory standards. Security and data privacy are critical, given the sensitive nature of financial data.
Overall, the system should balance automation with human oversight, enabling faster, more reliable, and scalable loan processing.

Requirements:
- Allow customers to submit loan applications online
- Automatically assess credit risk using predefined criteria
- Generate approval or rejection recommendations
- Provide a dashboard for loan officers to review applications
- Track application status in real time
- Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements (e.g., audit logs)

Dependencies:

- Dashboard depends on backend APIs
